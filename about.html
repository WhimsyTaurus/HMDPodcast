About us

Our Hosts

Derek Asklar

Derek has been metal detecting for 10+ years.

Craig Talley

Craig has been metal detecting for 20+ years.

Metal Detecting is more than digging dirt, it's a chance to escape into eutierria. It is a secular experience; your perception of the boundaries between yourself and others seems to evaporate.

HMD Radio is a podcast, with 500+ episodes,founded by two friends who share a passion for metal detecting and a respect for the environment.

HMD is more than metal detecting talk. It's a raw format podcast that runs roughly 60mins on Thursdays at 9:00 PM EST. HMD takes live call-in callers every show. They are always interested in hearing from noobies and professionals alike!

HMD is undergoing a bit of website, and podcast programming construction. We are currently off-the-air but will be returning to full on-air braodcastig shortly.
